Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Travel Content Studio is a local-first creator tool that helps users organize their own travel footage, create short-form video drafts, and optionally connect their own TikTok account.

Information We Process

The application may process video files, captions, content preferences, project information, and other material selected by the creator. Media and project data are stored locally on the creator’s device unless the creator chooses to use an optional connected service.

TikTok Connection

When a creator chooses to connect TikTok, Travel Content Studio uses TikTok authorization to access only the permissions approved by that creator. Depending on the permissions granted, this may include basic account information, the creator’s own public videos, and the ability to upload drafts or publish creator-approved videos.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to provide video organization, content creation, duplicate prevention, draft upload, publishing, and related creator workflows. Travel Content Studio does not sell TikTok data or use connected account information for advertising.

Data Sharing

Travel Content Studio does not share creator content or TikTok account data with unrelated third parties. Optional AI services may receive only the content or metadata necessary to provide a creator-requested feature.

Data Retention and Deletion

Creators control locally stored footage and project data. TikTok access can be revoked through TikTok settings or by disconnecting the account from the application.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the operator of Travel Content Studio through the support contact shown in the application.
